Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

App Name: Draw

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

This document describes how Draw handles user-related data and the rules for using our engagement-based reward service. By using Draw, you agree to this policy alongside any terms shown inside the app.

1. Data Collection and Usage

  • Identity Information: We collect your email address via Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In. This is used to secure your account, sync achievement profiles, manage event entries, and deliver rewards.
  • Device Identifiers: We process data such as IDFA (on iOS) and AAID (on Android) through Firebase and advertising SDKs only with your explicit consent (ATT for iOS). This helps prevent fraud and duplicate accounts.
  • Activity Data: We process in-app interactions, such as mini-game scores (2048, Sudoku, etc.) and engagement points, to maintain a fair global leaderboard and reward system. By participating in these activities, you consent to your score being displayed on our public leaderboards.

2. Third-Party Advertising and Analytics

  • Advertising SDKs: To keep our service free, we use Unity Ads, Google Mobile Ads, and mediation adapters. These providers may collect non-identifiable data to show relevant ads.
  • User Control: You can opt-out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings (e.g., iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking).

3. Achievement & Reward System Policy

  • Engagement Points: Users earn virtual points through in-app activities such as daily check-ins and logic games. These points are strictly promotional and have no real-world cash value until redeemed.
  • Reward Distribution: Points may be redeemed for digital gift cards (e.g., Amazon, Starbucks). All winners are selected through a transparent, random achievement-based simulation.
  • No Real Money Gambling: Draw does not offer real money gambling. Users cannot wager real money, and no purchase is necessary to participate in any event.
  • Skill-Based Platform: Our system is designed to reward user engagement and proficiency in our logic games library.

4. Apple and Google Disclaimer (Required)

Apple Inc. and Google LLC are not sponsors of, or responsible for, conducting any promotion or activity within the Draw app. All rewards and events are provided solely by the developer (Monoplain Labs).

5. Data Deletion and Privacy Rights

Your privacy is our priority. We encrypt all traffic via HTTPS. You have the right to request account and data deletion at any time through the App Settings or by contacting our support email. Once requested, we will purge all identifiable information from our servers within 30 days.
